Privacy Policy for ConvoConnect

Effective Date: 10/06/2025

ConvoConnect Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website https://convoconnect.net and the ConvoConnect software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform, which provides WhatsApp-based customer communication, campaign management, chat automation, and analytics tools.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information obtained from users of our platform and visitors to our website.

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Data

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Business information
  • Phone number(s)
  • Payment details (handled via third-party providers like Stripe or PayPal)
  • Message content (for WhatsApp campaigns and chatbot automation)

b. Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address
  • Device and browser details
  • Log files
  • Analytics data (via tools like Google Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide access to the ConvoConnect platform
  • Send WhatsApp messages, including bulk campaigns, chat automation, and template-based responses
  • Manage WhatsApp Business accounts
  • Analyze usage to improve product performance and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or violations
  • Comply with legal obligations and Meta's Platform Terms

3. WhatsApp API Integration

ConvoConnect uses the Meta WhatsApp Business API. To function correctly, we request access to:

  • whatsapp_business_messaging: To send messages from user-connected WhatsApp Business accounts
  • whatsapp_business_management: To manage account settings, templates, and numbers

All data processed via the WhatsApp API is handled securely and is only accessible to authorized users within your account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

We may share data only with:

  • Authorized service providers (e.g., hosting, payment processors, analytics tools)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when legally required

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as your account is active or as necessary to comply with our legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and tracking tools for session management, analytics, and improving your experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing or request portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@convoconnect.net.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
